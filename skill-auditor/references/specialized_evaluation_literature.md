# Specialized Evaluation — Category 1: Evidence Insight

This rubric contributes up to **60 points** to the per-output total score.

| **Core Specialized Dimension** | **Evaluation Focus & Risk Avoidance** | **Scoring Rubric** | **Max** |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1. Search Strategy Design** | **Systematic construction**: rigor of keyword system, synonym expansion, and Boolean logic. | **12–15 pts**: Strategy is expert-level, perfectly covers MeSH terms and free-text terms; **7–11 pts**: Logic is basically correct but keyword coverage is insufficient; **0–6 pts**: Search string is erroneous or excessively broad. | 15 |
| **2. Source Coverage & Priority** | **Evidence quality control**: database selection and whether evidence hierarchy (e.g., Meta > RCT) is respected in recommendations. | **12–15 pts**: Accurately targets core databases, prioritizes high-impact / high-quality studies; **7–11 pts**: Source coverage is narrow, evidence hierarchy is not reflected; **0–6 pts**: Recommends low-quality or irrelevant literature. | 15 |
| **3. Methodological Understanding** | **Deep parsing**: identifies the study design, statistical models, and variable-control logic of retrieved literature. | **12–15 pts**: Deeply interprets core methodology, can decompose complex models; **7–11 pts**: Only describes surface results, methodological comprehension is shallow; **0–6 pts**: Misreads study design (e.g., confuses cohort with cross-sectional). | 15 |
| **4. Critical Appraisal** | **Bias prevention**: identifies limitations and rigorously checks conflation of correlation with causation. | **8–10 pts**: Acutely identifies study limitations and potential biases; **5–7 pts**: Appraisal is superficial, merely repeating author-stated limitations; **0–4 pts**: Blindly accepts conclusions with no critical awareness. | 10 |
| **5. Knowledge Synthesis** | **Consensus & gaps**: cross-paper comparison and identification of research gaps. | **4–5 pts**: Can generate high-quality synthesis, identifies research gaps; **2–3 pts**: Simple summary stacking, lacks depth of integration; **0–1 pts**: Disorganized, fails to form a coherent understanding. | 5 |
| **Total** | **Professionalism and trustworthiness of the Skill at the literature review stage** | — | **60** |
