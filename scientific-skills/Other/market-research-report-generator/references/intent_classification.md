# Intent Classification Framework

Use this framework to categorize the user's research request.

## 1. Market Entry
- **Focus**: Feasibility, barriers to entry, regulatory landscape, initial capital requirements.
- **Key Questions**: "Is this market accessible?", "What are the legal hurdles?", "Who are the incumbents?"

## 2. Investment / Due Diligence
- **Focus**: ROI, CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate), risk assessment, exit strategies, valuation comparables.
- **Key Questions**: "Is this a growing market?", "What are the risks?", "Is the valuation justified?"

## 3. Product Strategy / R&D
- **Focus**: Feature comparison, user pain points, technology trends, patent landscape.
- **Key Questions**: "What features are missing in current products?", "What is the next tech breakthrough?", "What do users complain about?"

## 4. Competitive Intelligence
- **Focus**: Competitor SWOT analysis, market share, pricing strategies, marketing channels.
- **Key Questions**: "How are competitors pricing?", "What is their go-to-market strategy?"

## 5. General Education / Landscape
- **Focus**: Broad overview, history, key definitions, ecosystem map.
- **Key Questions**: "What is this industry?", "Who are the players?", "How does it work?"
