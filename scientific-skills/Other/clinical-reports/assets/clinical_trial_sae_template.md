## Notes

**Regulatory Timelines:**
- **Fatal or Life-threatening Unexpected SAEs:** Initial report within 7 days, complete report within 15 days
- **Other Serious Unexpected Events:** Within 15 days
- **IRB Notification:** Follow institutional policy (usually 5-10 days)

**Key Points:**
- Complete all sections accurately
- Provide a thorough narrative
- Include temporal relationship notes
- Document all sources of information
- Follow up until the event is resolved
- Protect patient privacy
- Use de-identified information only
