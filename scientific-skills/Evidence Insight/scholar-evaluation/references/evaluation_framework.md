# ScholarEval Framework

## Core Dimensions & Rubrics

### 1. Problem Formulation
*   **Definition**: Clarity, significance, and novelty of the research question.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Is the problem clearly stated?
    *   Is the motivation compelling?
    *   Does it address a gap in current knowledge?

### 2. Literature Review
*   **Definition**: Coverage and synthesis of relevant prior work.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Are key papers cited?
    *   Is the relationship to prior work clear?
    *   Does it avoid "laundry list" citation styles?

### 3. Methodology
*   **Definition**: Soundness and appropriateness of the approach.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Is the method technically sound?
    *   Is it appropriate for the problem?
    *   Is it reproducible?

### 4. Data Quality
*   **Definition**: Suitability, size, and bias of datasets used.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Is the data sufficient for the claims?
    *   Are preprocessing steps described?
    *   Are biases addressed?

### 5. Analysis
*   **Definition**: Depth and correctness of the analytical techniques.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Are statistical tests appropriate?
    *   Are baselines fair?
    *   Are ablation studies conducted?

### 6. Results
*   **Definition**: Clarity and significance of the findings.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Do results support the claims?
    *   Are improvements significant?
    *   Are limitations discussed?

### 7. Writing Quality
*   **Definition**: Structure, clarity, and grammar.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Is the logic easy to follow?
    *   Is the language precise?
    *   Are figures/tables clear?

### 8. Citations
*   **Definition**: Accuracy and formatting of references.
*   **Criteria**:
    *   Are citations accurate?
    *   Is the format consistent?

## Scoring Weights

| Dimension           | Weight |
|---------------------|--------|
| Problem Formulation | 15%    |
| Methodology         | 20%    |
| Analysis            | 20%    |
| Results             | 15%    |
| Literature Review   | 10%    |
| Data Quality        | 10%    |
| Writing Quality     | 5%     |
| Citations           | 5%     |
