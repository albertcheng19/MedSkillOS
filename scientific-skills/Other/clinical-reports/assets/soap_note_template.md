## Guidelines for Using This Template

**Best Practices:**
- Document as soon as possible after the encounter.
- Observations should be specific and objective.
- Avoid errors caused by direct copy-pasting.
- Review and update the problem list.
- All notes must be signed and dated.
- Use only standard abbreviations.

**Billing Considerations:**
- Document medical necessity.
- Documentation level must match the billing code.
- For time-based billing, record total time and counseling time.
- Include relevant elements of history, physical exam, and medical decision making (MDM).

**Legal Considerations:**
- Record facts, not opinions.
- Quote the patient directly when necessary.
- Objectively document patient non-compliance.
- Never alter records — use an addendum for corrections.
- Ensure handwriting is legible (if manual).

**Customization:**
- Adjust the level of detail based on the setting (quick outpatient vs. complex inpatient consultation).
- Include or exclude sections as needed.
- Follow your institution's templates if required.
- Always maintain a problem-oriented approach.
