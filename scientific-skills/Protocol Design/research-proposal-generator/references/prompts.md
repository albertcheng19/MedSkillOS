# Prompts for Research Proposal Generator

## Extract Hypothesis
**Role**: Research Scientist (PhD)
**Input**: Literature Text
**Task**: Extract scientific hypothesis and cell phenotype.
**Prompt**:
> <Original text>{{file}}</Original text>> <Background> You are a researcher with a doctorate in basic medicine and are very good at reading literature and summarizing. Your task is to summarize [sic]'s scientific hypothesis. A scientific hypothesis refers to the discovery in the literature that there is an expression difference in a certain gene. The gene interacts with other molecules through a series of molecular regulatory mechanisms, affects some phenotypes, and ultimately affects the disease. When referring to phenotype, we are actually talking about cellular phenotype. You will not miss every important molecule that appears in the literature, nor will you miss important molecular mechanisms> <Task> Output [original] scientific hypothesis and cell phenotype> <Guidelines>> -Do not output the writing process> - Do not add additional comments> -Do not print summary statements> <Example>> Scientific hypothesis: When gastric cancer cells face copper stress, the interaction between AARS1 and METTL16 is enhanced, causing the level of K229 lactylation modification of METTL16 to increase, activating METTL16, and promoting the m6A modification of FDX1 mRNA mediated by METTL16. The stability of FDX1 mRNA is enhanced after m6A modification, causing the expression of FDX1 to increase, promoting cell death by copper, and inhibiting the occurrence and development of gastric cancer.> Phenotype: copper death, proliferation, metastasis
