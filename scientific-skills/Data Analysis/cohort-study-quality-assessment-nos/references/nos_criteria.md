# Newcastle-Ottawa Quality Assessment Scale (NOS) for Cohort Studies

## 1. Selection

### 1.1 Representativeness of the Exposed Cohort
- **Goal**: Assess if the study is truly representative of exposed individuals in the community.
- **Criteria**:
  - Allocation one star (*) for the affirmative answer.
  - Negative or uncertain answer is marked with "-".
  - *Example*: Subjects derived from groups likely to contain middle class, better educated women are likely to be representative of postmenopausal estrogen users.

### 1.2 Selection of the Non-Exposed Cohort
- **Goal**: Assess if the study was drawn from the same community as the exposed cohort.
- **Criteria**:
  - Drawn from a different source or no description -> No star.
  - Allocation one star (*) for the affirmative answer.

### 1.3 Ascertainment of Exposure
- **Goal**: Was the study with secure record (e.g. surgical records) or structured interview?
- **Criteria**:
  - Secure record or structured interview -> One star (*).
  - Written self-report or no description -> No star (-).

### 1.4 Demonstration that Outcome of Interest was Not Present at Start of Study
- **Goal**: Verify outcome was not present initially.
- **Criteria**:
  - Affirmative answer -> One star (*).
  - Negative or uncertain -> "-".

## 2. Comparability

### 2.1 Age Comparability
- **Goal**: Were the age matched in the exposed and non-exposed?
- **Criteria**:
  - Either matched in design and/or confounders adjusted for in analysis.
  - Statements of "no differences" without adjustment are NOT sufficient.
  - Affirmative answer -> One star (*).

### 2.2 Additional Comparability
- **Goal**: Were other controlled factors (excluding age) matched?
- **Criteria**:
  - Matched in design or adjusted in analysis.
  - Affirmative answer -> One star (*).

## 3. Outcome

### 3.1 Assessment of Outcome
- **Goal**: Verify independent or blind assessment, or confirmation by secure records.
- **Criteria**:
  - Independent blind assessment, or secure records (x-rays, medical records), or record linkage (ICD codes) -> One star (*).
  - Self-report -> No star (-).

### 3.2 Enough Follow-up
- **Goal**: Was follow-up long enough for outcomes to occur?
- **Criteria**:
  - Acceptable length (e.g., 5 yrs for breast implants) should be decided beforehand.
  - Affirmative answer -> One star (*).

### 3.3 Adequacy of Follow-up of Cohorts
- **Goal**: Did the study have adequacy of follow up?
- **Criteria**:
  - Complete follow up -> One star (*).
  - Follow up rate >= 80% and description of lost -> One star (*).
  - Follow up rate < 80% or no description -> No star (-).
