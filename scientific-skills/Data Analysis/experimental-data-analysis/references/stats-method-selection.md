# Statistical Method Selection Quick Reference

## Comparison of Two Groups

- Normal distribution and homogeneous variance: Independent samples t-test
- Non-normal distribution: Mann-Whitney U

## Paired Design

- Paired t-test or Wilcoxon

## Comparison of Multiple Groups

- One-way / Multi-way ANOVA
- Post-hoc test: Tukey

## Correlation

- Pearson / Spearman
