# Peer Review Checklist

## Input Checklist

- Full paper and abstract
- Submission goals or review criteria

## Output Checklist

- Structured review report
- List of major and minor issues
- Recommended conclusion and rationale

## Output Format

- Includes Summary / Strengths / Weaknesses / Major / Minor.
- Numbered issue items for easy response.

## Quality Check

- Evaluation basis is clear and specific.
- Suggestions are actionable and verifiable.

## Common Issues

- Providing only conclusions without evidence or rationale.
- Suggestions are too vague.
