# Standard Discharge Summary Template

## Document Header
- Hospital Name
- Department
- Document Title: DISCHARGE SUMMARY
## Section 1: Patient Demographics
Required fields:
- Full name- Gender- Age- Medical record number (hospitalization number/medical record number)- Admission date- Discharge date- Length of stay (number of days in hospital)- Department- Attending physician
## Section 2: Admission Information
### 2.1 Chief Complaint- Patient's primary reason for admission
- Duration of symptoms
- Standard format: "Symptom + Duration"

### 2.2 Present Illness History- Detailed description of current illness
- Onset, progression, and treatment prior to admission
- Relevant positive and negative findings

### 2.3 Past Medical History- Previous medical conditions
- Surgical history
- Allergies
- Medications prior to admission

### 2.4 Physical Examination (admission physical examination)- Vital signs on admission
- Systematic examination findings
- Pertinent positive/negative findings

### 2.5 Admission Diagnosis- Primary diagnosis
- Secondary diagnoses
- Differential diagnoses if applicable

## Section 3: Hospital Course
### 3.1 Treatment Summary (Treatment Process)- Major therapeutic interventions
- Response to treatment
- Changes in clinical status

### 3.2 Procedures Performed (operation/operation record)- Surgical procedures with dates
- Diagnostic procedures
- Interventional procedures

### 3.3 Significant Findings (important inspection results)- Key laboratory results
- Imaging findings
- Pathology results
- Other diagnostic findings

### 3.4 Complications- Any complications during hospitalization
- Management of complications
- Outcome

### 3.5 Consultations (Consultation Records)- Specialist consultations
- Recommendations and actions taken

## Section 4: Discharge Status
### 4.1 Discharge Diagnosis (discharge diagnosis)- Final diagnoses (ICD-10 codes if applicable)
- Updated from admission diagnosis if changed

### 4.2 Discharge Condition (condition when discharged)- Patient's status at discharge
- Functional assessment
- Activity tolerance

## Section 5: Discharge Medications (discharge medication)For each medication:
- Generic name
- Dosage
- Route of administration
- Frequency
- Duration
- Special instructions

## Section 6: Discharge Instructions (discharge instructions)### 6.1 Follow-up Appointments- Scheduled appointments with dates
- Department/contact information
- Tests to be completed before follow-up

### 6.2 Activity Restrictions- Work restrictions
- Exercise limitations
- Lifting restrictions
- Driving restrictions

### 6.3 Diet Instructions- Specific dietary recommendations
- Fluid restrictions
- Special diet requirements

### 6.4 Warning Signs- Symptoms requiring immediate medical attention
- Emergency contact information
- When to call 911/120

### 6.5 Wound Care - if applicable- Dressing change instructions
- Signs of infection
- Shower/bathing restrictions

## Section 7: Physician Signature
- Attending physician printed name
- Signature
- Date
- Medical license number

## Quality Checklist
Before finalizing discharge summary:
- [ ] Patient identifiers correct
- [ ] Dates accurate
- [ ] Diagnoses match medical record
- [ ] Medication list complete and accurate
- [ ] Dosages verified
- [ ] Follow-up appointments confirmed
- [ ] Contact information provided
- [ ] Warning signs clearly stated
- [ ] Physician review completed
- [ ] Signature and date included

## Legal Considerations
- Document must be accurate and complete
- Any alterations must be initialed and dated
- Copy must be provided to patient
- Original filed in medical record
- Maintain patient confidentiality
